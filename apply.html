How to Apply

In order to join the 2D4U Organization, you must create a GitHub account. If you already have a GitHub account, click here to sign in. After you create your GitHub account, create a new issue on 2D4U's website repository. Please put your name, and an email to contact you. Write 4 to 5 sentances on why you would like to join and how you can be a positive addition to our group.
